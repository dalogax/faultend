4. Traffic logging

When recording is enabled on a fault server (the default), Faultend stores a copy of every HTTP request and response that flows through it, including headers, bodies, and timing data. Sensitive authentication headers (Authorization, Cookie, X-API-Key, Proxy-Authorization, X-Auth-Token) are redacted in the stored log — their values are replaced with [redacted]. Raw traffic is never forwarded to third parties.

Logs are retained for 7 days and then automatically deleted. You can clear them at any time from the server settings, or disable recording entirely.
